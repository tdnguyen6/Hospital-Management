About

TDQ Hospital is a multi-specialised healthcare facility located in Ho Chi Minh City's new urban development.

Over two decades of tradition and excellence in providing high quality medical care, and a skilled team of expert physicians, specialists and nurses in caring for the thousands of patients we treat daily. Our wide array of modern medical services continuously improves the health of the community around us, all under a safe, standardized and convenient environment.

At TDQ Hospital, the individual needs of our patients and their families are our top priority. As a leading healthcare organization in Vietnam, we aspire to be the healthcare destination of choice in HCMC and be recognized for having the most satisfied patients, the best possible clinical outcomes, and the most professional physicians, specialists and staff.
